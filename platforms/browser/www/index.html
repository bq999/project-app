Welcome to Lazeez!


We know you will love our treats. Order today, Satisfaction Guaranteed.

Lazeez bakery offers a vast range of tempting delights, traditional Lebanese pastries, baklava, mahmoule, Turkish delight, nougat, malban, sugared almonds, and many more. Our secret, one of a kind recipes are authentic and have been passed on for generations for you and your family to enjoy.

All our catering specialties are prepared with the same high-quality ingredients and care you enjoy every day in our bakery. Whether you are catering a business meeting, a social gathering or any other event, our catering coordinator is happy to assist you in customizing the perfect menu for breakfast, lunch or dinner.
